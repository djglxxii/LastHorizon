# Visual Distinctness

`visual-distinctness-reference.png` shows the new coalition fuel-cell carrier beside the existing weapon-chip carrier at gameplay scale.

- Fuel-cell carrier: cool blue/cyan utility hull with a bright fuel canister payload.
- Weapon-chip carrier: warm enemy/cargo hull with an amber chip pod.
- The silhouettes differ: the fuel-cell carrier has a compact utility hull and hanging canister; the weapon-chip carrier is wider, flatter, and enemy-coded.
